DIRECTLY PAYMENT EXPERIENCE

The main goal of this project is to enhance the payment experience for Directly users by creating a more intuitive and seamless checkout process. This redesign focuses on improving conversion rates, reducing cart abandonment, and providing a more secure and user-friendly payment experience across all devices.

Directly Payment Experience
Platform: Web & Mobile
Date: 2024

PROBLEM

Directly users face challenges with the current checkout process, including complex navigation, unclear payment options, and security concerns that lead to cart abandonment.

SOLUTION

A redesigned payment experience that simplifies the checkout process, provides clear payment options, and enhances security features while maintaining Directly's trusted brand identity.

TARGET MARKET

The target audience includes both new and existing Directly users who need a more streamlined and secure payment experience across all devices.
