THE STORY

I worked on the branding for crypto currency startup named Waterpark. Waterpark runs transparent, reliable and fun cryptocurrency mining pools. I started to design logo from scratch, because this startup didnt have any logo before. I worked remotely and used Figma for this project to be able to share the live link of my sketches and progress to the client. I ctreated couple variations of logos in black and white and then continue to work on next iterations. I comunicated with the client on every stage of this project.
